FAQ

What is Cheer Academy?

Cheer Academy is an online platform that offers
tutorials on boosting happiness, spreading joy,
and mastering the art of smiling.

How do I join?

Simply sign up for free on our website. You’ll
get immediate access to our vast collection
of tutorials and courses.

Are the courses free?

Yes, all our core courses are free. However,
premium courses taught by industry leaders
may require a membership.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Absolutely! Our courses are designed to
fit your schedule so you can learn whenever
it’s convenient for you.
